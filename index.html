This game* uses the names of episodes from Star Trek: The Original Series (sorry, TNG fans, maybe someday) and nail polish colours from the OPI and SpaRitual lines and forces you to consider which one is which.

* There are no scores, so it's not really a game. Or maybe it's not really a sport. I don't know.
